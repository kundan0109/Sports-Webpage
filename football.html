FOOTBALL




Facts of Football :

Oldest Football Club:
Sheffield FC, founded in 1857, is recognized as the world’s oldest football club by the Football Association and FIFA.
Pele’s Legendary Record:
Brazilian football legend Pelé is the only player to have won three FIFA World Cup titles (1958, 1962, and 1970).
First Ever World Cup:
The first-ever FIFA World Cup was held in Uruguay in 1930. The host nation, Uruguay, won the tournament by defeating Argentina 4-2 in the final.
